  • Broccoli Soup

    Ingredients:

    1. 1 tbsp olive oil
    2. 1 garlic clove chopped
    3. 250ml Vegetable Stock
    4. 200g Broccoli florets
    5. Slat and Pepper

    Method:

    1. Heat oil in pan for 1-2 minutes
    2. Pour vegetable stock and broccoli florets into pan
    3. Bring to boil and leave to simmer for 10 minutes
    4. Season with salt and pepper
    5. Serve!

  • Broccoli Pasta

    Ingredients:

    1. 12oz uncooked pasta
    2. 6 cups of fresh broccoli florets
    3. 5 cloves of garlic, smashed and chopped
    4. 1/4 cup of grated parmesan
    5. 2 tbspn olive oil

    Method:

    1. Boil pasta, once it has reached boil add the broccoli florets
    2. When the pasta is cooked, drain the pasta but reserve 1 cup of the water in the pot and return the pot to the stove
    3. Add the olive oil and the garlic to the water, cook until golden
    4. Add the pasta and broccoli back to the pot and mix well
    5. Add the cheese on top and serve!
